# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Engram to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone, regardless of
experience level, background, or identity.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that helps:
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what's best for the project and its users.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, trolling, publishing
others' private information, and other conduct that would reasonably be considered
inappropriate.

## Scope & enforcement

This applies in all project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions) and when representing the
project publicly. To report an issue, open a private security advisory or contact the
maintainer through the repository. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject
contributions and comments that violate this code, and may ban contributors for
egregious or repeated violations.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
